Our Courses

Quran memoization

The Quran Memorization (Hifz) Course is an opportunity for students to deepen their connection with the Quran by memorizing its verses and chapters. Through this course, students will not only develop their memorization skills but also gain a deeper understanding of the Quranic text and its teachings.

Learn More
Tajweed (Quranic Recitation)

The Tajweed (Quranic Recitation) Course is an opportunity for students to improve their Quranic recitation skills by learning and applying the rules of Tajweed. Through this course, students will not only develop their recitation skills but also gain a deeper appreciation for the beauty and melody of Quranic recitation.

Learn More
Tafsir (Exegesis)

The Tafsir (Exegesis) Course is an opportunity for students to deepen their understanding of the Quran by studying its interpretation and commentary. Through this course, students will not only develop their analytical skills but also gain a deeper appreciation for the diversity of interpretations within the Islamic tradition.
